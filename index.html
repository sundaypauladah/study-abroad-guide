🎯 Your Step-by-Step Process
1

Pick your destination region first

Start with the Universities tab. Europe (Germany, Norway, Italy, Poland) = free or very low tuition. Asia (Malaysia, India, Philippines) = affordability + English. Anglophone (UK, Canada, Ireland, Australia) = post-study work visas.

2

Check scholarship deadlines before admission

This is the most critical rule. Chevening (UK): Nov 2. SISS Sweden: Feb 10. Stipendium Hungaricum: Jan 15. Eiffel France: Jan 10. Missing them means waiting a full year.

3

Apply for government scholarships first

Go to 🏛️ Govt Scholarships. These cover tuition + living + flights. Top picks for NGN/GHA/KEN: Chevening, DAAD, Stipendium Hungaricum, GKS Korea, MEXT Japan, CSC China, ICDF Taiwan, Australian Awards.

4

Look at school scholarships and grants

Go to 🎓 School Awards. Italy's DSU/ER.GO grants can waive 100% tuition AND pay a monthly stipend. APU Japan can waive up to 100%. Many UK universities give automatic partial awards on admission.

5

Master's or PhD? Consider TA/GA positions

Teaching and Graduate Assistantships in USA/Canada often include 100% tuition waiver + $1,200–$2,500/mo stipend. Mention TA interest in your Statement of Purpose. Netherlands PhD positions are salaried employment contracts.

6

Use student loans only as a last resort

💳 Student Loans: MPOWER covers 500+ US/Canadian schools (no cosigner). Prodigy Finance covers 1,800+ schools in 19 countries for Master's. Lendwise covers UK. Use Radius to pay tuition without FX delays.

7

Prepare documents 6–12 months early

APS (Germany): 6–8 weeks — start by April for October intake. IELTS/TOEFL: book 3–6 months ahead. CampusFrance: complete before any French university application. Universitaly: register before Italian visa application.

📖 Key Terms
⚠️ Critical Warnings
⚠️
Scholarship deadlines are earlier than you think. Chevening: Nov 2. SISS: Feb 10. Stipendium Hungaricum: Jan 15. Eiffel: Jan 10. Miss them = wait a full year.
⚠️
Germany requires APS — start early. Cannot apply to any German university without it. Processing takes 6–8 weeks. Plan October intake? Apply by April.
⚠️
Italy regional grants are income-based. DSU/ER.GO/ESU grants require proof of household income (translated and notarised). Apply as soon as portals open in March.
⚠️
Brazil teaches in Portuguese. CELPE-Bras B2 language test required for PEC-G scholarship applicants.
⚠️
Tuition payment delays can cost your place. Use Radius to pay tuition internationally without FX delays — built specifically for this problem.
